What is a Cricut Machine?

Cricut is a brand of digital plotting cutting machine. They are easily available and the main digital cutting machine on the UK market.

Types of Cricut Machine

There are three types of Cricut Machine.

  • Cricut Joy

    1. The smallest machine
    2. Cut width 4 inches
    3. Single fine point blade, not compatible with Cricut Quickswap Blades

  • Cricut Explore

    1. Cut width 11.5 inches
    2. Three Different blade types, for cutting a wider range of material than the Cricut Joy.
    3. Not compatible with Cricut Quickswap Tools

  • Cricut Maker

    1. Cut width 11.5 inches
    2. Five Different blade types, for cutting a wider range of material than the Cricut Explore.
    3. Compatible with Cricut Quickswap Tools.

The Cricut Maker has the strongest cutting settings and can cut the largest variety of materials.
