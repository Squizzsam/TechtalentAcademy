Blades and Accessories

A Cricut Maker comes with a Fine Point Blade. This blade can intricately cut Vinyl, Paper and Card.
You can purchase additional blades for a Cricut Maker

  • Deep Point Blade
  • Bonded Fabric Blade
  • Rotary Blade
  • Knife Blade

The Cricut Maker also makes use of the QuickSwap Tools.

These use a Quickswap housing, but can also be purchased as interchangeable heads.
  • Scoring Wheel & Double Scoring Wheel
  • Debossing Tip
  • Engraving Tip
  • Perforation Tip
  • Wavy Tip
